Detailed Description

Implements mass-based dynamics for growth rate of organisms.

GrowthRateDynamics is a subclass of MassBasedDynamics and calculates the growth rate based on environmental conditions, including temperature, moisture, and relative humidity. The implementation follows the model described in Savage et al. 2004, Fig. 4B.
